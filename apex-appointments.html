📋 How Appointment Scheduling Works
▶
1
Carrier submits appointment request by 9 AM
Must be submitted within 24 hours of receiving the Container Available for Pickup notification.
2
Apex confirms or proposes alternative by 12 PM
Apex responds no later than the next business day (Mon–Fri, 8 AM – 5 PM local). If an alternative is offered, it must be on or before the Last Free Date.
3
Carrier reviews confirmation by 3 PM
Check the My Appointments page for status. If confirmed, you're all set. If an alternative was proposed, proceed to step 4.
4
Carrier reschedules and resubmits by 5 PM
Reschedule the terminal appointment to match Apex's alternative (or next available date), then submit a new request here with the updated dates.
5
Apex closes out open appointments by 5 PM
All remaining open requests are reviewed and finalized at end of day.
⚠️ Adhoc changes: Must be requested ≥4 hours before the scheduled appointment via email to amazon-oi@apexglobe.com and AmazonUSKAM@apexglobe.com, CC iypoorni@ and hsubrian@. Propose 2+ alternative time slots.
